Governed The profile above was assembled by the exchange gateway, not by giving the requesting agency direct access to the underlying databases. Every reach across agency lines goes through the same single, auditable surface.

  • Authenticated request. The requesting agency is identified by the X-Requesting-Agency header and recorded with each response (this lookup is attributed to minister-dashboard).
  • Rate-limited & auditable. Calls flow through the OpenChoreo data plane, where rate limits, quotas, and access policy are applied centrally — not implemented inside each service.
  • Zero-trust between agencies. The taxpayer-registry and returns systems are not directly reachable by the requesting agency; they are private to the project and only the gateway can compose their data.
  • Sovereign, on-premises stack. The whole platform runs in-country on open-source components — no proprietary control-plane and no vendor lock-in.
